SOS Emergency Alert
One-Touch Safety
Our SOS Emergency Alert system allows you to quickly send your location to emergency contacts with just one tap. Your safety is our priority, and we're here to help you feel secure wherever you go.
How It Works
When activated, the SOS alert will send your real-time location to your emergency contacts, along with a distress message. It can also trigger an automatic call to local emergency services if enabled in your settings.
